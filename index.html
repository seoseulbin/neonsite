Description

"NEON" is a top-down action-adventure game that offers a unique experience of survival on a dark planet. The defining concept of the game lies in the players' need to navigate through darkness by utilizing the brief trail of light left by the neon glow. "NEON" transcends simple light effects to become a crucial element of gameplay, posing a unique challenge for players to find the right balance between light and darkness, safety and danger.
The game offers over an hour of gameplay time, complete with 10 distinct, handcrafted stages and a full soundtrack. Each stage unfolds with a broader and increasingly beautiful pattern of neon waiting for you.
A new beginning of a great journey, "NEON" awaits you.

Will you join us on this grand journey?

Gameplay Mechanics

Objective

Find and exit through the portal in the dark and limited field of vision

The Challenge

Avoid touching the unique patterns of "neon" that illuminate the game space, posing a unique gameplay dynamic of balancing light and darkness, safety and danger.

Power-Ups

Collect coins throughout the stage, which can be used to purchase unique items in the store that appears every second stage

How To Play?

The gameplay is really simple! All you have to do is find and move through the exit portal in the dark and limited field of vision. Easy, right?
But be careful!
Each stage creates unique patterns of "neon" that you must avoid touching!
Make use of them!
The "neons" have a glowing attribute. This glow illuminates a certain range around the "neon," temporarily revealing the locations of items, exits, and obstacles on the stage!
Don't forget to make purchases!
The coins collected through the stages can be used to purchase items at the store that appears every second stage! There are nine unique items waiting for you!

Features

"NEON" boasts a unique gameplay experience with 10 distinct levels and a complete soundtrack. Players must navigate the dark environment utilizing the neon glow while avoiding obstacles and finding the right balance between light and dark.
